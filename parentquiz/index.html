Common questions:

  • Where is my data stored? Everything you enter stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.
  • How do I delete my data? Delete individual family members in the Family tab, or uninstall the app to remove everything.
  • Reminders not showing? Check Settings > Notifications > Parent Quiz and make sure notifications are allowed.

Privacy Policy

Parent Quiz does not collect any personal data. All information stays on your device.

Overview

Parent Quiz is developed and published by Enemies By Nature LLC. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

Parent Quiz operates entirely on your device. All family member information, birthdays, anniversaries, and quiz scores are stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework and never leave your device.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not use third-party tracking or advertising SDKs
  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not access your contacts or calendar
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade any information

Local Notifications

Parent Quiz uses Apple's local notification system to remind you of upcoming birthdays and anniversaries. These notifications are scheduled and processed entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

Data Storage

All data you enter into Parent Quiz (names, relationships, dates, notes) is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework. This data is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Included in your iCloud backup if you have device backups enabled
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app

Children's Privacy

Parent Quiz does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Third-Party Services

Parent Quiz does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features, including:

  • Device passcode/Face ID/Touch ID
  • iOS data encryption
  • App sandboxing

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal information for us to access, modify, or delete. All your data is under your control on your device. You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
